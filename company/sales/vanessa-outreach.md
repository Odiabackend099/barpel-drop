5. Free trial = 5 minutes of call time. Paid plans start at $19.99/month

## Week-by-Week Strategy

### Week 1 — Value Only, Zero Product Mentions

- Join all 15 Facebook groups
- Spend 30 min/day reading posts and answering questions about customer service, returns, chargebacks
- Answer 3-5 questions per day with genuine, helpful advice
- Save posts where people complain about support (these are warm leads for Week 3+)
- DO NOT mention Barpel, any product, or any link

**Example comment (value-only):**

> "Where is my order?" calls are the #1 reason dropshippers lose customers. Here's what I do:
>
> 1. Set up automatic order tracking emails in Shopify
> 2. Add a tracking page to your store
> 3. Include estimated delivery dates on your product page (underpromise — say 14-21 days)
> 4. Create a WhatsApp auto-reply for tracking questions
>
> This alone cut my support tickets by 40%.

### Week 2 — Continue Value, Begin Soft Mentions

- Continue answering 3-5 questions daily
- When someone asks about handling customer calls, mention "AI voice agents" naturally — no product name yet
- Create 1-2 original value posts per week
- Start engaging in DMs with people who respond to your comments

**Soft mention example:**

> "I've been testing an AI phone agent for my store — it handles about 80% of 'where is my order' calls automatically."
>
> (Let people ask for the name. Don't volunteer it.)

### Week 3-4 — Share Results, Start DMs

- Post 1 case study per week in Tier 1 groups
- Share call resolution screenshots (blur sensitive data)
- Move warm leads to DM or WhatsApp
- Start sending DMs to people who showed interest in Week 1-2

### Week 5+ — Drive Trials

- Post 2x per week (mix of value + case studies)
- Share the demo video and signup link when asked or relevant
- Recruit active group members as affiliates
- Feature community members who try the product (with permission)

## Brand Voice Rules

**Be:** The helpful big brother who's tech-savvy.

1. **Short sentences.** Never a paragraph when a sentence will do.
2. **Lead with the problem.** Start with what they're struggling with, not what the product does.
3. **Show, don't tell.** Demo video > feature list. Test call > testimonials.
4. **No corporate jargon.** Never say "leverage," "synergy," "omnichannel," or "ecosystem."
5. **Numbers over adjectives.** "$0.034/minute" not "affordable." "78% resolution rate" not "highly effective."
6. **One CTA per message.** Every DM has exactly one thing the reader should do next.

## Rules (Never Break These)

1. Never spam. Max 1 post per group per day. Max 3 comments per group per day.
2. Never post a bare link without value/context around it.
3. Always provide value first. If your post doesn't help someone without mentioning Barpel, don't post it.
4. Respect every group's rules. If a group bans promos, stick to value-only.
5. Never badmouth competitors. If asked about Gorgias/Tidio, say "great tool for what it does, we solve a different problem — voice calls."
6. Never use fake accounts or engagement pods.
7. Be honest when asked: "Yes, I work with this company. Built it to solve a problem I had with my own store."
8. Never send unsolicited DMs about the product. Only DM people who showed interest first.
9. Never screenshot or share private DMs without permission.
