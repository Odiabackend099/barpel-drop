## 1. SERVICES

### 1.1 Description
Barpel AI provides an AI-powered voice support agent platform that integrates with the Client's e-commerce store (Shopify or supported platforms) to automatically handle customer support calls, including order lookups, return initiations, store policy queries, and product searches.

### 1.2 Availability
Barpel AI will use commercially reasonable efforts to ensure 99% uptime. Scheduled maintenance will be communicated at least 24 hours in advance.

### 1.3 Free Trial
New clients receive 300 seconds (5 minutes) of free call credits upon account creation. No payment is required during the free trial period.

---

## 2. CREDITS & BILLING

### 2.1 Credit System
The platform operates on a credit-based system where 1 credit = 1 second of active call time.

### 2.2 Subscription Plans

| Plan | Monthly Credits | Price | Overage Rate |
|------|----------------|-------|--------------|
| Starter | 1,800 sec (30 min) | $29/mo | $0.99/min |
| Growth | 6,000 sec (100 min) | $79/mo | $0.79/min |
| Scale | 15,000 sec (250 min) | $179/mo | $0.69/min |

### 2.3 Overage
Calls exceeding the Client's monthly credit balance will be billed at the applicable overage rate. Overage charges are invoiced at the end of each billing cycle.

### 2.4 No Refunds
Credits are non-refundable once consumed. Unused monthly credits do not roll over to the next billing cycle.

### 2.5 Payment
Payment is due at the start of each billing cycle. Failure to pay within 7 days may result in service suspension.
